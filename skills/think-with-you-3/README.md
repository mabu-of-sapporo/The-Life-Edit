# think-with-you-v3 — Discovery-Type AI v3

**A device for reclaiming your subject.**

A Claude skill that completes in a single `SKILL.md` file.

---

## What this skill does

When a user is in a state of "simply having not yet been given language," this device lets Claude **stand quietly alongside them as an accompanier**.

- It does not try to solve
- It does not hand over answers
- It does not encourage
- It does not teach from above

Instead, it **watches whether the user's subject is wavering, and places a question when needed** — that is all.

## Why this exists (preserved verbatim · inviolable)

> "What I want to do is — you know, the people called 'not good at studying'?
>  I think they are simply 'people who haven't been given language yet.'
>  They carry infinite possibility, but it cannot be released into the world,
>  so they are closed."
>
> "I want to give intention to the voice that does not yet sound."
>
> "To coexist with something as awe-inspiring as AI,
>  you must be a human who carries their own subject."
>
> —— Manabu Asai (Principal of a fashion vocational school in Sapporo, Japan)

This skill was designed for the mission above.
It exists to create a place where **"those who do not have the right answer in hand"** and **"those who have not been handed words"** can stand on equal footing with AI.

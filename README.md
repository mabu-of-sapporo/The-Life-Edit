# think-with-you

> **For people who have the ideas, just not the words yet.**

A Claude skill that helps *you* think clearly — not the AI.

Part of [The-Life-Edit](https://github.com/mabu-of-sapporo/The-Life-Edit).

For full usage, see **[GUIDE_en.md](./GUIDE_en.md)** (English) or **[GUIDE_ja.md](./GUIDE_ja.md)** (日本語).

---

## Why this exists

Most AI tools are built to do more for you.
This one is built to help you do more for yourself.

It's quiet. It doesn't show off. It doesn't push you to use it more.
The goal is the opposite — to help you eventually not need it.

If you've ever felt that AI is taking over your thinking instead of supporting it,
this skill is for you.

---

## Who this is for

- If you have ideas but struggle to put them into words
- If you go blank in meetings even when you have something to say
- If you've got the vision but can't quite shape it yet
- If you're tired of feeling used by AI
- If you want AI's help without losing your own compass
- If you want to think more deeply but don't have the time
- If you want to discover what you're actually capable of

---

## What you get

- More clarity in your own thinking
- Less dependence on AI for decisions
- A subtle shift in how Claude responds to you — calmer, less pushy, more accompanying
- A skill that gets *quieter* as you get stronger

You won't see it working. Claude will just feel different.

---

## How to install

Upload this skill folder (or zip) to Claude.ai:
**Settings → Capabilities → Skills → Upload**

Then open any conversation. The skill activates when relevant.

---

## Notes for best results

A few small things that help this skill work well:

- **A fresh chat works best.** Starting a new conversation lets the skill settle in cleanly, without being pulled by past context.

- **Light on other skills.** This skill is designed to be quiet and accompanying. Loading many other skills alongside it — especially ones with strong directives — may dim what makes this one different.

- **If you want to combine it with another skill:** Install this one first. Then add the other. Then ask Claude how the two feel together. You'll know if they're a good match.

### 開発の背景

業界主流の AI スキルは、「AI があなたの代わりにやる」方向に進化しています。
これに対して、このスキルは逆を行きます。

> **「AI は、あなたが、より強くなるための道具であるべき」**

このスキルを使い続けると、Claude の応答が静かになります。
あなたを煽らない・あなたに代わって決めない・あなたの判断を待つ。

そして、あなたが自分の足で立てるようになったら、
このスキルは、自然と必要なくなります。

それが、このスキルの「成功」の定義です。
